How To Play

Phone players: rotate your phone sideways before you hit Start.

Run Tony Move, jump, and stay alive.

Watch Bullets Jump, dodge, or get whacked.
Mobile Controls

Top half: tap to jump

Bottom left: hold to walk left

Bottom right: hold to walk right

Hold a side + tap the top with another finger to jump while walking.

PC / Keyboard

← / →: walk left and right

Space or ↑: jump

P: pause
